Events & Community

Our Book/Library Events...

  • Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday we have our afterschool homework/studying program (this is for students who don't have a much access to wifi or technology at home so they finish their homework).
  • On the 1st Thursday of evey month we have our book club where all is welcomed (We have our teens and adults version!) And of course all is welcome as long as they sign up first.
  • Once a month we always have a special event to give back and help our community (We will be releasing information on the event for this month in a few days so stay tuned for that!)
  • Last but not least, keep an eye out for any new and exciting upcoming events for the community!
